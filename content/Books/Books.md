When I was in 7th grade, I broke my tablet, and since my parents couldn't afford a replacement, I felt incredibly frustrated. To pass the time, I reluctantly started reading a book from _**The Adventures Of Oz**_ franchise. Initially, it was a struggle—boring and difficult to focus on, much like starting a slow new TV show. But then, to my surprise, I became completely engrossed, unable to put the book down. This unexpected passion led me to discover some of the world's best novels, including _The Adventures of Tom Sawyer_ and a title from the St. Clare's series. However, my reading habit eventually faded when I acquired my mother's old phone; I donated my collection to the school library, but now I'm eager to reignite that cherished hobby.
