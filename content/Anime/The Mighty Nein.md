# Season 01
So far, I really enjoyed it. While **[[The Legend of Vox Machina]]** had some cringey moments due to it’s over-the-top humor, **The Mighty Nein** fixed this by maintaining a more serious tone with well-placed jokes.


**Rating: 5/4.8**

 - **4.8:** The art style and writing are both amazing.
 - **0.2:** A few episodes felt slightly slow-paced.
